Maggi Goreng

Description

This dish is a delicious vegetarian sauce for pasta, so tasty, and as nice as a meat bolognese. It can also be served with potatoes.

Ingredients required:

  • Olive oil
  • Onions
  • 2 Packets of Maggi
  • Carrots
  • Soy sauce
  • Eggs
  • Mushrooms

Step-by-step cooking guide:

  1. Boil instant noodles for 2 minutes. Do not fully cook it. It just needs to be in loose strands. In a large bowl, mix all the sauce ingredients until well combined and set aside until ready to use.
  2. Heat wok with 1 tbsp of oil. Saut√© garlic until aromatic. Add in chye sim and cabbage. Stir fry till they are slightly softened. Toss in the noodles to combine. Add in cooked potato cubes, tempeh and slowly add in the sauce. Using a tong, stir fry while tossing all the ingredients together until well-combined.
  3. Your Maggi Goreng is ready for consumption
