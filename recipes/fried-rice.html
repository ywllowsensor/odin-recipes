Fried Rice

Description

"Kampung" is the Malay word for village. Hence, Kampung Fried Rice is also known by many as Village-style Fried Rice. The origins of this dish can be traced back to the small attap houses in Malaysia and Indonesia. It has a spicy yet flavoursome palette that is bound to leave one yearning for more.

Ingredients required:

  • White rice
  • Olive oil
  • Chilli paste
  • Onions
  • Fried anchovies

Step-by-step cooking guide:

  1. Add 2 tbsp of olive oil onto a frying pan at medium heat
  2. Dice 1 onion while the oil heats up
  3. Add the diced onions into the pan
  4. Add 2 tbsp of chilli paste into the frying pan
  5. Add 200g of rice into the pan and stir fry well
  6. Stir fry for 20 minutes until the rice turns to a light brown hue
  7. Transfer the fried rice onto a plate and add 1 tbsp fried anchovies
  8. Your Kampung fried rice is ready for consumption
